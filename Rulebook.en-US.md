# Chromatyx – Rulebook

*A fast-paced color-shedding card game in outer space for 2 to 8 players, ages 6 and up.*

## Overview

In Chromatyx the players are pilots racing to unload their cargo: whoever
sheds all hand cards first wins. Twelve colors represent celestial bodies of
our solar system – from the yellow Sun to black Pluto – and every card
carries its astronomical symbol.

## Components

265 playing cards plus the card back:

| Cards | Count | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Planet cards | 190 | 10 colors, each with digits 0–9, action cards and +2/+3 traps |
| Rainbow cards (black, ♇) | 39 | Color-change traps, some with extra effects |
| Guard cards (white, ★) | 36 | Protection, mirroring, redirection and discards |

The ten planet colors:

| ☉ | ☿ | ♀ | ⊕ | ☾ | ♂ | ♃ | ♄ | ♅ | ♆ |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sun | Mercury | Venus | Earth | Moon | Mars | Jupiter | Saturn | Uranus | Neptune |
| Yellow | Purple | Pink | Green | Gray | Red | Orange | Brown | Cyan | Blue |

## Goal

Be the first to discard all of your hand cards.

## Setup

1. Shuffle all 265 cards thoroughly.
2. Each player receives **7 hand cards**.
3. The remainder forms the face-down **draw pile**.
4. Flip the top card to start the **discard pile**. If it is not a digit
   card, shuffle it back into the pile and flip a new one.
5. The youngest player starts; play proceeds clockwise.

## Playing the Game

On your turn, **play exactly one matching card** onto the discard pile or
**draw one card** from the draw pile. A drawn card may be played immediately
if it matches – otherwise the next player is up.

A card matches when it shares with the top card of the discard pile

- its **color**, **or**
- its **symbol** (same digit or same effect).

**Rainbow cards (black)** always match; whoever plays one chooses the color
the game continues with. **Guard cards (white)** follow their own rules (see
below).

When the draw pile runs out, reshuffle the discard pile except for its top
card.

## Card Types

### Digit Cards (0–9)

No effect – the bread and butter for changing colors and numbers.

### Action Cards

| Symbol | Name | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 🔄 | Reversal | Reverse turn direction |
| 🚫 | Block | Next player sits out |
| 🔂 | Repeat | Next turn is same player again |
| ❄️ | Freeze | Keep color for one full round |
| 🔥 | Burn | Color is banned for one full round |
| 🎯 | Focus | Freely choose the next player |

With **Freeze**, all players may only play the frozen color (or matching
black/white cards) for one full round; whoever cannot, draws. With **Burn**,
the Burn card's color is locked until the player who burned it is up again –
so the next card must match by symbol or be black/white.

### Trap Cards

| Symbol | Name | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| +2 / +3 | Penalties | Next player draws 2 or 3 cards |
| 🌈 | Rainbow | Current player chooses the color |
| +4🌈 | Rainbow +4 | Next player draws 4 cards, choose the color |
| 🚫🌈 | Rainbow Block | Next player sits out, choose the color |
| 🔄🌈 | Rainbow Reversal | Reverse turn direction, choose the color |
| 🔄+4🌈 | Rainbow Reversal +4 | Both together, choose the color |
| 🔄🚫🌈 | Rainbow Reversal Block | Both together, choose the color |
| 🎯+2🌈 | Rainbow Focus +2 | A freely chosen player draws 2 cards, choose the color |

Whoever must draw penalty cards and does not react (see Guard cards) draws
them and skips that turn.

### Guard Cards

The white star cards are your life insurance amid the chaos:

| Symbol | Name | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 🛡️ | Shield | Nullify all effects |
| 🛡️🔄 | Mirror | All effects hit the previous player |
| 🛡️🎯 | Focus | All effects hit a freely chosen player |
| −3 / −4 | Discard | Discard 3 or 4 cards |

**Shield, Mirror and Focus** are played **outside your own turn**: the
moment an action or trap card's effect hits you, you may immediately discard
one of these cards. A **Shield** cancels the effect entirely; **Mirror** and
**Focus** pass it on with all consequences to the new target – who may in
turn protect themselves. Afterwards play continues normally.

**Discard cards (−3/−4)** are played on your own turn instead of a regular
card: discard them together with 3 or 4 additional hand cards of your
choice.

White cards never need to match color or symbol.

## End of the Game

Whoever discards their last card wins immediately. The remaining players may
continue for the runner-up placings.

## Variants

- **Point Chase:** At the end of a round, every player tallies their
  remaining hand (digit = face value, action/trap = 20, Rainbow = 50,
  Guard = 50). Lowest total after several rounds wins.
- **Chromatyx!:** Whoever plays their second-to-last card must call out
  "Chromatyx!". Forget it and get caught – draw 2 penalty cards.
